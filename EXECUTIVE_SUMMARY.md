## What It Is
A unified platform that transforms customer product adoption from a manual, reactive process into an automated, data-driven growth engine.

## The Problem We Solve
- **65% of software features** go unused by customers
- **3-6 months average** implementation delays per product
- **40% of CSM time** spent on manual status tracking
- **Zero visibility** into adoption progress across bundled solutions
- **Manual processes** that don't scale with customer growth

## How We Solve It
**DAP provides:**
1. **Smart Adoption Planning**: Customized implementation roadmaps with automatic task filtering by license tier
2. **Automated Progress Tracking**: Real-time telemetry integration with automatic status updates
3. **Solution Bundle Management**: Unified tracking across multiple products with aggregated progress
4. **Operational Efficiency**: Bulk Excel import/export, drag-and-drop prioritization, consistent UI/UX

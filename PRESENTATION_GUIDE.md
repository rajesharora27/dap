## Presentation Flow (45 Minutes)

### **Opening (5 minutes)**
- **Hook**: "What if we could double CSM capacity without hiring?"
- Frame the problem in business terms (revenue, retention, efficiency)
- State your ask upfront: "Today I'm asking for approval to run a 60-day pilot"
- Outline agenda: Problem ‚Üí Solution ‚Üí Demo ‚Üí Business Case ‚Üí Next Steps

### **The Problem (5 minutes)**
- Focus on pain points executives care about:
  - Lost revenue (delayed time-to-value)
  - Operational inefficiency (CSM capacity)
  - Risk (churn due to poor adoption)
  - Competitive pressure (competitors offering better onboarding)
- Use statistics: "65% of features unused", "3-6 months delays"
- Make it personal: "Our CSMs spend 15 hours/week on status updates"

### **The Solution (5 minutes)**
- High-level overview (avoid technical jargon)
- Focus on outcomes, not features:
  - ‚ùå "GraphQL API with React frontend"
  - ‚úÖ "Automated tracking that saves 20 hours per CSM per month"
- Show the three pillars:
  1. Automated planning
  2. Real-time tracking
  3. Solution-level visibility
- One sentence: "We built a platform that does for adoption what Salesforce does for sales pipeline"

### **Live Demo (10 minutes)**
**Golden Rule**: Show, don't tell. Keep it simple.

**Demo Script:**
1. **Dashboard** (1 min): "Here's our customer list with adoption visibility"
2. **Product View** (2 min): "This is a product with 30+ tasks, licenses, outcomes"
3. **Adoption Plan** (3 min): "Customer assigned ‚Üí customized plan ‚Üí progress tracking"
4. **Solution Bundle** (2 min): "Multiple products unified ‚Üí aggregated progress"
5. **Telemetry** (1 min): "Status updates automatically from product usage"
6. **Excel Export** (1 min): "Bulk updates via familiar Excel workflow"

**Demo Tips:**
- Use real-looking data (avoid "Test Customer 1")
- Show before/after (manual spreadsheet vs. platform)
- Highlight the "wow" moments (auto-updates, real-time progress)
- Have a backup if live demo fails (recorded video or screenshots)

### **Business Case (10 minutes)**
**Lead with ROI:**
- "10 CSMs √ó 20 hours/month saved √ó $50/hour = $120K/year"
- "Investment: $10K first year ‚Üí 1200% ROI"
- "Payback in under 3 months"

**Quantified Benefits:**
| Benefit | Current State | With DAP | Impact |
|---------|---------------|----------|--------|
| Planning time | 8 hours/customer | 3 hours/customer | 60% reduction |
| Status updates | 15 hours/week/CSM | 7 hours/week/CSM | 50% reduction |
| Accounts per CSM | 10 | 20-30 | 2-3x capacity |
| Time-to-value | 6 months avg | 3.5 months avg | 40% faster |

**Strategic Value:**
- Competitive differentiator in sales process
- Foundation for predictive analytics
- Scales with customer growth (no linear headcount increase)
- Customer experience improvement (professional onboarding)

### **Risk & Mitigation (5 minutes)**
**Address concerns proactively:**
- "Is this another tool that won't get adopted?"
  - ‚Üí Pilot with champions, prove value first, make it easier than current process
- "What if it doesn't work?"
  - ‚Üí 60-day pilot with clear go/no-go criteria, full data export capability
- "Do we have capacity for this?"
  - ‚Üí Minimal time investment (4 hours training), immediate time savings
- "What about integration complexity?"
  - ‚Üí Start manual, phase in automation, Excel workflow as fallback

**Show confidence:**
- Platform is production-ready (not vaporware)
- Zero technical debt
- Built with modern, proven technologies
- Already tested with realistic data volumes

### **The Ask (5 minutes)**
**Be specific:**
1. "Approve 60-day pilot program starting in 2 weeks"
2. "Assign 3-5 CSMs to pilot team (ideally volunteers)"
3. "Select 3-5 pilot customers (diverse license/solution mix)"
4. "Budget approval: $10K/year for infrastructure"
5. "Executive sponsor to support change management"

**Define success:**
- Measurable: 30% reduction in planning time
- Qualitative: Positive CSM feedback
- Data: 5 customers fully tracked with accurate metrics
- Decision: Clear go/no-go after 60 days

**Create urgency:**
- "Q4 is prime time to pilot (before year-end push)"
- "Competition is investing in adoption tech"
- "Our customers are asking for better visibility"
- "Every month of delay = $10K in lost CSM productivity"

### **Q&A (10 minutes)**
See "Handling Objections" section below

### **Close (5 minutes)**
- Recap key points: Problem ‚Üí Solution ‚Üí ROI ‚Üí Low-risk pilot
- Restate the ask: "Can we proceed with pilot approval today?"
- Thank the team
- Clarify next steps and decision timeline
- Leave handouts (executive summary)

‚úÖ **Speak their language**: Revenue, retention, efficiency, ROI
‚úÖ **Show don't tell**: Live demo > slides

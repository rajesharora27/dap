## Slide 9: Implementation Plan

### **Path to Production**

**Phase 1: Pilot Program (Month 1-2)**
- Select 3-5 high-value customers
- Load existing products and adoption plans
- Train 2-3 CSMs on platform usage
- Gather feedback and iterate

**Phase 2: Team Rollout (Month 2-3)**
- Migrate all active adoption plans
- Full CSM team training
- Establish data import/export workflows
- Begin telemetry integration

**Phase 3: Optimization (Month 3-4)**
- Analyze adoption patterns and bottlenecks
- Refine task weights and success criteria
- Implement automated reporting dashboards
- Integrate with CRM/data warehouse

**Phase 4: Scale & Expand (Month 4+)**
- Roll out to all customer-facing teams
- Enable self-service customer access (future)
- AI/ML predictive analytics (future)
- Mobile app for field teams (future)

**Resource Requirements:**

## General Design Principles

### **Typography**
- **Headers**: Sans-serif, bold, 36-48pt (Montserrat, Roboto, Open Sans)
- **Body**: Sans-serif, regular, 18-24pt
- **Callouts**: Bold or colored, 24-30pt
- **Captions**: Light, 14-16pt

### **Color Palette**

**Primary Colors:**
- Dark Blue: #1A365D (trust, stability)
- Vibrant Blue: #3182CE (innovation, tech)
- White: #FFFFFF (clean, clear)

**Accent Colors:**
- Green: #38A169 (success, growth)
- Purple: #805AD5 (premium, future)
- Orange: #DD6B20 (urgency, action)
- Red: #E53E3E (problems, risks)

**Usage:**
- Blue: Main content, positive elements
- Green: Success, ROI, completed items
- Orange: CTAs, urgency, warnings
- Red: Problems, risks (before state)

### **Imagery Guidelines**

**Do's:**
âœ… High-resolution (at least 1920x1080)
âœ… Consistent style (all illustrations OR all photos)
âœ… Relevant to content (no generic stock photos)
âœ… Professional and polished
âœ… Accessible (good contrast, not distracting)

**Don'ts:**
âŒ Low-quality or pixelated images
âŒ Cheesy stock photos (people pointing at screens)
âŒ Clip art or dated graphics
âŒ Text-heavy images (hard to read)
âŒ Watermarked images

**Recommended Sources:**
- Unsplash (free high-quality photos)
- unDraw (free illustrations)
- Heroicons / Material Icons (icons)
- Your own dashboard screenshots

### **Data Visualization Best Practices**

**Charts:**
- **Bar Charts**: Comparisons, before/after
- **Line Charts**: Trends over time, ROI progression
- **Pie Charts**: Avoid (use bar charts instead)
- **Gauges/KPIs**: Single metrics, progress indicators
- **Waterfall Charts**: Cost breakdowns, ROI buildup

**Chart Design:**
- Simple, clean axes
- Clear labels and legends
- Consistent colors across slides
- Highlight key data points
- Remove chart junk (unnecessary gridlines, 3D effects)

### **Layout Best Practices**

**Spacing:**
- Generous white space (don't cram)
- Consistent margins (50-100px)
- Align elements to grid
- Group related content

**Hierarchy:**
- Most important info: Top-left or center
- Supporting details: Below or right
- CTAs: Bottom-right or center-bottom

**Consistency:**
- Same font sizes for similar content
- Same icon style throughout
- Same color meanings across slides
- Same layout structure where possible

## PowerPoint Template Setup

### **Master Slide Elements**

**Title Master:**
- Company logo (top-left or top-right)
- Slide number (bottom-right)
- Date (bottom-left, optional)
- Confidential footer (centered, small)

**Content Master:**
- Title area (top, left-aligned)
- Content area (centered, 70% of slide)
- Footer with logo, page number, confidential notice

**Section Master:**
- Large section title (centered)
- Icon or image
- Minimal text

### **Slide Transitions**
- Use sparingly (fade or none)
- Consistent across all slides
- No animations on data slides (distracting)
- Optional: Subtle fade-in for bullet points

## Export & Delivery

### **File Formats**
- **PowerPoint (.pptx)**: Editable, preferred for business
- **PDF**: Non-editable, good for distribution
- **Google Slides**: Collaborative, cloud-based
- **Keynote (.key)**: Mac users, high design quality

### **Distribution Checklist**
- [ ] Slide deck (PPTX or PDF)
- [ ] Executive summary (1-page PDF)
- [ ] ROI calculator (Excel)
- [ ] Demo access (link or video)
- [ ] Contact information
- [ ] Appendix materials (zip file)

### **Presentation Day**
- Test on actual presentation equipment
- Have backup (USB, cloud link, PDF)
- Print handouts (executive summary)
- Arrive early to set up
- Test clicker/remote if using
